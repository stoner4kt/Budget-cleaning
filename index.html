Our Cleaning Services

Comprehensive cleaning solutions for homes and properties

Deep Cleaning

Our Deep Cleaning service is a comprehensive, top-to-bottom clean designed for homes that need extra care and attention. This service goes beyond regular cleaning and focuses on built-up dirt, grime, and hard-to-reach areas that are often overlooked. Ideal for first-time clients, homes that haven't been professionally cleaned in a while, or before special occasions.

Spring Cleaning

Spring Cleaning is perfect when your home needs a full refresh and reset. This service helps eliminate dust, allergens, and cluttered dirt that accumulate over time, leaving your space feeling lighter, fresher, and more organised. Ideal for seasonal changes, year-end cleans, or whenever your home needs a fresh start.

Student Accommodation Cleaning

We offer reliable and thorough cleaning services for student accommodation, including shared houses, apartments, and residence-style living. This service is ideal for end-of-lease clean-outs, room turnovers, or general cleans for existing students. A practical solution for landlords, parents, and students who need fast, effective cleaning at an affordable rate.

Post-Renovation Cleaning

After renovations or building work, dust and debris can settle throughout the home. Our Post-Renovation Cleaning service removes construction dust, paint splashes, residue, and leftover materials to make your home safe and livable again. We clean walls, floors, windows, surfaces, fixtures, and fittings with care.

Pre & Post-Occupation Cleaning

Our Pre- and Post-Occupation Cleaning service ensures a property is spotless before new occupants move in or after previous occupants move out. This service is ideal for tenants, landlords, and property owners who want a smooth handover. We focus on kitchens, bathrooms, cupboards, floors, and all living spaces.

Window Cleaning

Interior and exterior window cleaning for crystal clear views and enhanced natural light in your home.

Our Story
