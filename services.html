Deep Cleaning Cape Town

Our deep cleaning service is more detailed and intensive than a Spring Clean. It covers your entire home from top to bottom, focusing on detail, hygiene, and hard-to-reach areas. Perfect for homes that need extra attention, first-time professional cleans, or before special occasions and events.

What's Included in Our Deep Cleaning Service:

  • All floors thoroughly vacuumed and mopped throughout the property
  • Skirting boards, door frames & window frames meticulously cleaned
  • All surfaces wiped down and sanitised with professional products
  • Light switches, plug points and wall plates cleaned throughout
  • Kitchens: cupboards (outside), countertops, sinks, stove, oven, microwave cleaned. Inside cupboards cleaned if empty and accessible
  • Bedrooms: furniture wiped down, beds moved to clean underneath. Inside cupboards cleaned if empty and accessible
  • Living & dining areas: furniture and couches wiped, vacuumed, and moved to clean underneath where possible
  • Bathrooms: toilets, basins, showers, baths, tiles, walls, and vanities thoroughly scrubbed and sanitised
  • Removal of cobwebs, dust buildup, and grime from all areas
  • Deep scrubbing of grout, tiles, and hard-to-reach corners

Benefits of Professional Deep Cleaning:

  • Eliminates hidden dirt, dust, and allergens for healthier indoor air quality
  • Ideal for allergy sufferers and families with young children or pets
  • Extends the life of your flooring, furniture, and fixtures
  • Creates a fresh, hygienic living environment
  • Perfect preparation for property inspections, viewings, or special events

Post Renovation Cleaning Cape Town

After renovations or building work, dust and debris can settle throughout the home. What's Included in Post-Renovation Cleaning:
